CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

DIRECTIONS

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below, clicking on a crystal will add to your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly chosen number.
You take the L if your total score goes over the randomly chosen number.

Each time the game starts, the randomly chosen values of each crystal will change.

Let's start collecting!
